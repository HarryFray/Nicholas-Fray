About me

I’m Nick Fray, a full stack Software Engineer with extensive experience in JavaScript, using such technologies as React, Redux, Node.js, MongoDB, and GraphQL to name a few. I love working in creative team environments and building applications that have the potential to effect positive change upon the world. Wow, what a sentence, but a true one. When I’m not building websites i’m happiest reading a good book, camping, or having a coffee with close friends.
